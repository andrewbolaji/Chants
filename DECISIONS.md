# Decisions

## 2026-07-02: Ink and Gold palette retune

Palette moved from warm-brown surfaces to the Ink and Gold direction:
- Neutral deep-charcoal cards (#161616, #1C1C1C), deeper near-black base (#060606)
- Gold accent brightened to #FFC02E, used on brand and interactive moments (VERIFIED sticker, club name, vote highlight, shuffle icon)
- Subtle gold print-echo shadow added to card titles (1px offset, 30% gold opacity)
- Search box reduced to ~78% footprint (icon 16px, font 12px, tighter padding)

Visual only, no logic or schema change. Chosen mockup-first and approved. The warm surfaces read as a muddy low-contrast wash; Ink and Gold sharpens legibility while keeping the Fanzine loud-frame-calm-words direction.
